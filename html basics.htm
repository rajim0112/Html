The Tortoise and The Hare

Once upon a time, there was a hare who loved to boast of his speed in front of the other animals.
All the animals were surprised that the tortoise took up the challenge. He was known to be a very slow animal. However, a day was fixed for the great race and all the animals looked forward to it.On the day of the race, no animal went to the market.
No one went hunting, all the animals gathered together, excited to watch the race between the tortoise and the hare. Both animals were ready, each of them felt confident and everyone wondered why the tortoise was so confident since they felt he was no match for the hare.
