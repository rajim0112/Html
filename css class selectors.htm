The bustling marketplace overflowed with vibrant sights and intoxicating smells. Strings of colorful lanterns swayed in the warm breeze, casting dancing shadows on the crowd below. The air buzzed with a cacophony of sounds: haggling vendors, excited chatter, and the rhythmic clanging of metal on metal from a distant blacksmith.
